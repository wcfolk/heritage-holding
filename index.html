Woodcraft Folk Heritage

After 10 years of success - the Woodcraft Heritage Website's funding has come to an end. This unfortunately means that the Heritage Website as it was has had to be switched off. We are eternally grateful to the Heritage Lottery Funding and to the generous Groups & Districts who funded the website in 2015. We hope to one-day bring back the heritage website when funding allows, sign up to our E-Newsletter below to be the first to know of any updates.

100 Objects

“100 Objects for 100 Years” project will showcase our stories throughout 100 years of Woodcraft. Funded by the National Lottery Heritage Fund, we will uncover the incredible history of the Woodcraft Folk, spanning the world with friendship!
